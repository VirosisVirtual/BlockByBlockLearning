Welcome to Nivora-IV

Survive the frozen tundras of an eternal ice world. Prepare for harsh conditions and test your skills as a planetary explorer.

Your Mission

As the sole survivor of a crash landing, your objective is to establish a sustainable outpost on Nivora-IV. Harness geothermal energy, construct habitats to withstand extreme temperatures, and adapt to a barren environment where resources are scarce.
